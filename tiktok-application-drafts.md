## Scopes Explanation (1000 char max)

Peak Overwatch is an analytics dashboard that helps TikTok Shop affiliate creators monitor and optimize their performance.

**User Info (user.info.basic):** Used during OAuth login to identify the connected TikTok account. We display the user's display name and avatar on their dashboard profile. This lets users confirm which account is connected and manage multiple account connections.

**Video List (video.list):** Used to retrieve the user's published videos and display them in a sortable content library. Users can see their full video catalog, filter by date range, and identify which content is driving the most engagement and affiliate sales.

**Video Insights (video.insights):** Used to pull performance metrics (views, likes, comments, shares) for each video. This data powers our analytics charts, trend detection, and performance alerts. Users receive notifications when engagement drops below their configured thresholds, helping them respond quickly to FYP changes.

All data access is read-only. We never post, modify, or delete content on behalf of users.
